Last updated: January 16, 2026

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the core functionality of the App (finding nearby places), we collect and process the following information:

  • Location Data (GPS): We access your device's precise location solely to search for places of interest near you and to display your position on the map. This data is transmitted to our servers to fetch relevant content but is not stored permanently or used to track your movement history.

2. Use of Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:
